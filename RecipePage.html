Chicken Alfredo

This is a simple and delicious recipe for Chicken Alfredo.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute.
  3. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 6-7 minutes per side. Remove from skillet and slice.
  4. In the same skillet, add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce, tossing to coat. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve the pasta topped with sliced chicken.
  7. Enjoy your Chicken Alfredo!
